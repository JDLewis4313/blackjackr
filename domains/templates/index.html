How to Play Blackjack

- The goal is to reach 21 without going over.
- Face cards are worth 10.
- Aces are 1 or 11.
- Each player starts with 2 cards.
- Hit to draw another card, Stand to keep your total.
- Dealer must draw until reaching 17.
- Closest to 21 wins!
